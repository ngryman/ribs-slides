It is a period of vendor wars.

The evil empire Microsoft © is dominating the browser market. With the help of their massive destruction weapon, Internet Explorer, they are now the masters of the Web.

People are now subjects to bugs, extreme slowness and precarious technology.

This is the dark age of the Web...

Fortunately a group of rebels have used their best strategy to prevent a rughtless domination of The Empire. They have released the source code of their browser, Netscape.

Using the collaboration of many resistant, they have now developed the most powerful weapon against tyranny, Firefox.

This terribly successful strategy is called Open Source.

The Open Source now rules the Web, and The Empire is now far less powerful. It is even doing Open Source itself in order to survive this new order.

This is now the golden age of the Web.
